Sakae PR (USA) and Sakae Cultural (Brazil) was founded by Juliana Sakae — a publicist-producer with a career built at the intersection of curatorial instinct and strategic precision. Her expertise was forged managing the competition leading to the prestigious IDA Awards and creating the Bravo FYC within the Bravo Film Festival.

We operate across the full spectrum of the prestige cinema circuit: awards campaigning, international festival positioning, press strategy, and educational initiatives through our Masterclass program. Our approach is singular: we treat each film as a cultural object with a story to tell beyond the screen.

We work exclusively with projects we believe in — which is why our record speaks for itself.
